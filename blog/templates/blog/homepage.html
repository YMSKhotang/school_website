About us

What do you want to know?

YouMe School Khotang is an non-profitable organization that provides educational support to help pave the way for the future of children in khotang, Nepal.This organization was founded by its representative, Sharad Rai, when he was a university student with the thoughts of ``I want to open up a future for the children of my home country of Nepal!'' and ``I want to give back to my home country that has raised me!''

Our Founder

Namaste. My name is Sharad Rai and I am the representative of the NPO YouMe Nepal. Although I currently live in Japan and worked for a Japanese company, I was born and raised in Nepal.he village of Durchhim in khotang District, where I was born, is located in a very rural area in Nepal. It was the kind of place where I had to spend an hour and a half every day to fetch water . It was a really empty place, so I often practiced whistling while riding on top of a water buffalo. There were also Bengal tigers in the area, so I couldn't go into the forest carelessly.When I was in the fourth grade of elementary school, I was selected as one of only 99 scholarship students selected every year from all over Nepal, and was able to receive a proper education in the capital, Kathmandu, with government money.Thanks to that, after graduating from high school, I was able to study abroad at a Japanese university on a scholarship, and got a job in Japan. However, most of my classmates in my hometown did not graduate from high school. All of them go abroad to work as manual laborers. why is it? That's because they haven't received a proper education.

~ Sarad Rai
